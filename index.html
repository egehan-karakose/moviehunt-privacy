Privacy Policy for MovieHunt

Last updated: July 2025

Privacy First: MovieHunt does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from users.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

MovieHunt collects NO personal data.

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Language preferences (stored locally)
  • Game progress and scores (stored locally)
  • App settings (stored locally)

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

MovieHunt uses the following third-party services that may collect their own data:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad serving purposes according to their own privacy policy:

Google Privacy Policy

3. DATA STORAGE

All user preferences and game data are stored locally on your device using Flutter's secure storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Can be cleared by deleting the app

4. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Since MovieHunt collects no personal information, it is safe for users of all ages including children under 13.

5. YOUR CONTROL

You have full control over your data:

  • All data stays on your device
  • Delete the app to remove all data
  • Control ad preferences through your device settings
  • No account required, no data to delete

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in app updates with a new "last updated" date.
